
Tales of Claude - Roadmap Verification Report

**Roadmap Verification Specialist Report - Session 3 Audit**

**Date:** 2025-06-24
**Game:** Tales of Claude (React/TypeScript 2D RPG)
**Workflow:** REVOLUTION (AI Agents)
**Audit Scope:** Comparison of actual implementation post-Session 3 against planned roadmap (Sessions 4-20) and Chris's Wishlist.

---

**EXECUTIVE SUMMARY:**

The REVOLUTION workflow has delivered an impressive amount of functional core systems and content in a remarkably short timeframe. Session 3, "The Quest Revolution," appears to have been a significant success, delivering a robust quest and faction system. Phase 2, "Living World Achieved," also successfully implemented time, weather, enemy AI, minimap, and puzzles.

However, there's a critical disconnect between the *claimed* completion of "The Great Expansion" goals in CLAUDE.md (Session 3 roadmap) and the *actual* state as described in CLAUDE_KNOWLEDGE.md and the Master Roadmap for Sessions 4-20. Specifically, the "BIGGER MAPS" objective, a cornerstone of Chris's vision and a stated Session 3 goal, has been largely deferred to Session 4. This over-statement, while potentially a result of ambitious planning versus practical execution, needs to be addressed for roadmap clarity and stakeholder alignment.

The Master Roadmap (Sessions 4-20) is ambitious but generally well-structured, building logically on the *actual* strong foundations. The immediate priority must be to rectify the "BIGGER MAPS" discrepancy and ensure Session 4 truly delivers on this long-standing request.
